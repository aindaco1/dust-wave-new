La Grima wins multiple awards at 2022 Albuquerque 48 Hour Film Project

We won Best Performer, Best Cinematography & Sound, Audience Award Group C, and 2nd Best Film at 2022 Albuquerque 48 Hour Film Project.


After another 48 hour dash of creating, performing, shooting, editing, and submitting our climate drama, La Grima, we won a bunch of awards!

  • Best Performer (John Dewar)
  • Audience Award, Group C
  • Best Cinematography & Sound
  • 2nd Best Film
