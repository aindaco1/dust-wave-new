SLAC Fund Terms

Overview

The SLAC (Supporting Local Artists of Color) Fund supports aspiring writer/directors of color in New Mexico. Each selected filmmaker receives a support package worth $2,500, including tools, crew, fundraising help, and access to Dust Wave's community — leading to a premiere screening at The Guild Cinema in Albuquerque.

What Filmmakers Receive



Production Support

  • Access to camera, sound, and lighting gear (as available)

  • Assistance with location scouting

  • Access to crew from Dust Wave's network

  • Guidance through our Notion-based production system

  • Shot lists, calendars, budget tracking, and task management

Fundraising & Budgeting

  • Crowdfunding support, including:

  • Campaign strategy

  • Page content and video pitch guidance

  • Launch & promo calendar

  • Assistance organizing a local fundraising event

  • Support for budget creation and oversight

Post-Production & Strategy

  • Assistance with editing, sound, and color

  • Festival strategy, including:

  • Application timelines

  • Platform suggestions

  • Submission letter assistance

  • Marketing support, such as:

  • Flyer/social media templates

  • Press release help

Final Showcase & Visibility

  • Guaranteed premiere screening at the Guild Cinema

  • Promo through Dust Wave's platforms

  • Opportunity for Q&A and community celebration

  • Continued exposure through Dust Wave's network


Eligibility



  • Applicants must:

  • Identify as an aspiring writer/director of color

  • Be based in or able to work in New Mexico

  • NOT be a current member of Dust Wave

  • Have a short film concept (5–15 mins preferred)

  • Be available to commit 20 hours/week to the project

  • Be open to collaboration and feedback


Participant Commitments



  • To receive full SLAC Fund support, filmmakers must:

  • Meet all deadlines in pre-pro, production, and post

  • Complete all required admin tasks (budgets, call sheets, updates)

  • Dedicate 20 hours per week to the project

  • Honor Dust Wave values — see full list here, including:

  • Collaboration

  • Mutual respect

  • Joy in process

  • Integrity

  • Cultural humility

  • Collective responsibility

Credit Requirement



  • All completed films must include the following line in the end credits:

  • “This film was made possible with support from the SLAC Fund”

  • All completed films must include the following organizations in a Special Thanks section of the credits:

  • Dust Wave

  • Cultivating Coders

  • Nusenda Credit Union

Marketing Requirement



  • All completed films will be featured on the Dust Wave website (dustwave.xyz).

  • Stills and clips from the film may be used to promote Dust Wave and Dust Wave events/projects.

Terms of Removal



  • Support may be revoked if a participant:

  • Fails to meet expectations or deadlines

  • Drops below time commitment

  • Acts in ways that conflict with Dust Wave's values
