Gumshoe wins multiple awards at the 2023 Albuquerque 48 Hour Film Project

We won Best Poster, Best Trailer, and Best Acting Ensemble at the 2023 Albuquerque 48 Hour Film Project.


After yet another 48 hour dash of creating, performing, shooting, editing, and submitting our sci-fi noir short, Gumshoe, we won a bunch of awards!

  • Best Poster, Group B
  • Best Trailer, Group B
  • Best Acting Ensemble
