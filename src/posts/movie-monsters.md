_Movie Monsters_ is a kaiju comedy short created in partnership with [Space Age Daydream](https://www.instagram.com/spaceagedaydreamfilms). Shot at Studio 519 and MOOD Studios in Albuquerque, New Mexico.
</br>
</br>
