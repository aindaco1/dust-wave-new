_Tower of Cower II_ was created for [City Brights III](https://www.cabq.gov/artsculture/public-art/events#tower-of-cower-ii), Albuquerque's temporary public art program for downtown light-based installations.

## Details

**Artists:** Alonso Indacochea, Adrian Pijoan, and Becca Elbrecht

**Location:** Lobby of Studio 519, 519 Central Ave NW, Albuquerque, NM 87102

**Launch:** December 5, 2025 during ArtWalk

**On view through:** April 6, 2026

## About the Work

_Tower of Cower II_ takes the form of a multi-level screen sculpture constructed from vintage CRT and LED televisions, some functioning and others gutted and fitted with mirrors that scatter light outward. The screens cycle between short silent films on migration and surveillance, plus live footage of visitors captured in real time. The tower becomes both watcher and witness, projecting stories of border crossings while reflecting the gaze of its audience back onto itself.

Created for City Brights III, the installation joined a downtown corridor of temporary public artworks activating Albuquerque after dark through projection, sculpture, and light.
