Welcome to this week’s *DIY Filmmaker Digest* — part syllabus, part mixtape, part fever dream of cinema’s shifting ground. Below: essays and interviews that ask what artists are tinkering with, what systems are breaking, and how you might improvise your own way forward. There’s a **major** New Mexico bias, plus podcasts, videos, and trailers to keep your queue weird. **DIY forever!**

</br></br>
<center><img src="/img/news/revolver.png" class="w-75 shadow-1-strong rounded mb-2" alt="worst movie ever"></center>
</br>
</br>

But first, a little congratulations: [The Worst Movie Ever](https://pool.dustwave.xyz/campaigns/worst-movie-ever/) is funded! This project is a weird one -- **we're not just crowdfunding but also crowdsourcing.** Each and **every backer gets to contribute something that the filmmakers must include in the film!** So for example, you can **[buy a frame of the movie for $1](https://pool.dustwave.xyz/campaigns/worst-movie-ever/#tier-frame)** -- make the frame whatever you want ([within reason](https://pool.dustwave.xyz/terms/)). **Buy 24 and get a whole second of the movie!** Or (my personal favorite) **make the filmmakers [shoot one of the scenes in the language of your choice](https://pool.dustwave.xyz/campaigns/worst-movie-ever/#tier-language)** for the **low, low pledge of $1000!** _Anyone speak Klingon?_

</br>
<center><img src="/img/news/klingon.gif" class="w-75 shadow-1-strong rounded mb-2" alt="worf concerned"></center>
</br>
</br>

The Worst Movie Ever is the **pilot project for [The Pool](https://pool.dustwave.xyz)**, Dust Wave's very own open-source crowdfunding platform -- more on that later I promise. And we're not even raising money for the production costs -- we're making this old-school for $0 and 0 cents (as much as that's possible) and giving the money to one of our dearest friends and partners, [FUSION](https://www.fusionnm.org/)! So if you hate us, cool -- make our lives miserable by **[buying 20 fart sound effects](https://pool.dustwave.xyz/campaigns/worst-movie-ever/#tier-sound-effect).** And if you love us, **[do the same.](https://pool.dustwave.xyz/campaigns/worst-movie-ever/#tier-sound-effect)** If we crack $5K, the filmmakers (anonymous as of now) have promised to **get a [Worst Movie Ever tattoo!](https://pool.dustwave.xyz/campaigns/worst-movie-ever/#stretch-goals)** _So please make them do something very permanent and stupid!_
